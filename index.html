About Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful landlocked country located in South Asia, mainly in the Himalayas. It is home to Mount Everest, the world’s highest peak, and Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Kathmandu
  • Population: ~30 million
  • Official Language: Nepali
  • Currency: Nepalese Rupee (NPR)

Highlights

Nepal is famous for its natural beauty, diverse cultures, and adventure tourism including trekking, mountaineering, and rafting. The country is also rich in temples, festivals, and traditions.

Fun Fact

Did You Know?

Nepal’s flag is the only national flag in the world that is not rectangular in shape!
